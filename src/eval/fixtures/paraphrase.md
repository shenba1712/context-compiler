# Village gossip

Jane often stayed with friends after dances. Netherfield was mentioned at
supper. Nobody here records why she remained after she got sick, only that
she liked the neighbourhood and stayed for the company.

# Travel notes

Falling rain delayed coaches. Ill horses were swapped at the inn. Jane’s
name appears once in a passenger list for Netherfield road.

# The letter

My dearest Lizzie, I find myself very unwell this morning, which I suppose
is to be imputed to my getting wet through yesterday. My kind friends will
not hear of my returning home till I am better.
