# Critical Notes on Early Drafts

Scholars writing early on about this period often mention Jane and the Bennet
family in passing, but these notes never record what anyone thought of her.
They discuss early manuscripts, early reviews, and early on the author's habits.

# Neighbours Gossip

Everyone talked about Jane Bennet at the assembly. Mr. Bingley danced twice.
Nobody here quotes his opinion of her looks.

# Chapter Opinion

Between him and Darcy there was a steady friendship. Bingley had never met with
pleasanter people. As to Miss Bennet, he could not conceive an angel more beautiful.
Darcy acknowledged her pretty, but said she smiled too much.
