## Chapter 2: Refund policy

Refunds are processed within 14 business days once the item is returned
in original condition. Shipping fees are non-refundable except when the
seller made an error.

## Chapter 3: Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 90 days written notice.
Immediate termination is allowed for material breach that remains uncured
after fifteen days.
