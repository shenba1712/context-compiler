# The Harrington Ball

Miss Harrington arrived late. Everyone whispered about Mr. Harrington's estate.
The orchestra played until midnight. Miss Harrington danced with several partners
and left without giving her card.

# Chapter Correspondence

The morning post brought several letters. One bore the Harrington seal.

Miss Harrington wrote often to her sister about the season. She complained of
the heat and the crowds. Nothing in her letters used her Christian name.

# The Invitation Reply

We should be delighted to receive you on Thursday. The gardeners have prepared
the roses. Yours sincerely,

EMILY HARRINGTON.

The butler placed the note on the silver tray.
