The quarterly compliance briefing was circulated to all managers last Tuesday
without a table of contents. Staff are reminded that visitor badges expire
after eight hours and must be returned at the lobby desk.

Separately, the finance team confirmed that expense reimbursements for travel
are paid within twenty-one calendar days of an approved claim. Receipts older
than ninety days are rejected automatically by the portal.

Parking permits for contractors are issued on Mondays only. Lost permits
incur a replacement fee of forty dollars. Building maintenance continues
overnight on the second floor this month and elevators may be offline.

Remote workers must complete the annual security quiz by March 31. Incomplete
quizzes block VPN renewal. The quiz covers phishing, password managers, and
device encryption baselines used across the company.
