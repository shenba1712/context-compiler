# Product Manual

## Warranty

Dropping the unit in water voids the warranty immediately. Physical damage
from misuse is also excluded. Keep the original receipt for claims.

## Battery and travel

Lithium batteries under 100Wh may be carried in cabin luggage on most
airlines. Spare batteries must be individually protected against short
circuit. Do not check spare lithium packs in hold luggage.

## Weather flying

The drone should not be flown in rain, snow, or winds above 10 m/s.
Light drizzle still risks water ingress at the motor hubs.
